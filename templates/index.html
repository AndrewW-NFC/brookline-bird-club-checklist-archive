About This Prototype

This site is a browser for existing Brookline Bird Club records. The underlying data reflects decades of club record keeping, historical trip documentation, eBird checklist entry, and the club statistician's long-running work to collate and preserve those records.

The software here does not replace that work. It is an exploratory layer for searching, browsing, and discussing how the club's records might be presented on a future records site.

Useful caveat: Some historical checklists use broad eBird locations, such as county-level or route-style locations. In those cases, checklist comments may describe the actual places visited better than the formal eBird location field.
